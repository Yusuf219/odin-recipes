Honey Garlic Salmon

home

Description

Salmon with white rice and veggies.

Get your rice pot ready and honey drizzling for some honey garlic salmon.

Ingredients

  • 250g of salmon
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian herbs
  • 1 tablespoon of honey syrup
  • A pinch of salt and pepper
  • A cup of rice
  • A handful of veggies

Steps

  1. With the salmon seasoned, place into pre-heated oven at 200C for 21 minutes
  2. Boil the rice and veggies in two pots and stir well
  3. Season the salmon with honey syrup and serve
